Overview

This is inspired by the rich, timeless traditions of India, especially local artisans who have inherited their craft from generations gone by. We endeavor to do our part in enriching the lives of our extremely-talented artisans, who despite their skill and craft, still struggle to make ends meet and live a decent life. India is a country that has since time immemorial known to be rich in arts and crafts.

Artisans are the backbone of the non-agricultural rural economy, and most of them are common folk, living below the poverty line. One of the biggest challenges of our time is to ensure that their skill, which is unique and invaluable in a sense, remains relevant and alive.
